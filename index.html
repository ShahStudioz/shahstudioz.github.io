01

Overview

▶

This Privacy Policy applies to the Android application Sentry – Remove Image Metadata (the "App"), developed as a free, independent project.

Sentry is a local utility tool that strips EXIF, XMP, IPTC, and other embedded metadata from your photos. All processing occurs entirely on your device. We are committed to protecting your privacy — which is straightforward here, because we collect nothing.

By using the App, you agree to the terms described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use.

02

Information We Collect

▶

We collect no information whatsoever. The App does not gather, store, transmit, or share any personal data, device identifiers, usage statistics, analytics, crash reports, or any other form of data.

✗ No personal data ✗ No analytics ✗ No crash reports ✗ No device identifiers ✗ No usage tracking ✗ No location data

The images you process remain entirely under your control. They are read locally, processed on-device, and saved to a new album in your gallery — no copy is ever transmitted anywhere.

03

Android Permissions

▶

The App requests only the minimum permissions required for its core functionality. Below is a full breakdown of each permission and exactly why it is needed:

Permission Purpose
READ_MEDIA_IMAGES
(Android 13+)		 Allows you to select images from your gallery for metadata removal. Access is granted only to images you explicitly choose.
READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
(Android ≤ 12)		 Legacy equivalent for reading images on older Android versions. Same scope — only images you select.
WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
(Android ≤ 9)		 Required on older Android versions to save the cleaned images to the output gallery album.

No permissions are used beyond their declared purpose. No background access, continuous monitoring, or cross-app data sharing occurs.

04

How Your Images Are Processed

▶

When you select images in Sentry, the following process occurs entirely on your device:

① Select images ② Strip metadata locally ③ Save to new gallery album

Original images are never modified. A clean copy — with all or selectively chosen metadata removed — is written to a dedicated album in your gallery. The App does not cache, copy, or retain any image data between sessions.

Supported metadata that can be removed includes EXIF data (GPS location, camera model, date/time, device info, orientation), XMP properties, IPTC fields, and other embedded technical or identifying information.

05

Internet & Network Access

▶

Sentry does not use the internet. The App declares no INTERNET permission in its Android manifest. It is architecturally incapable of transmitting any data over a network.

✓ Fully offline ✓ No network requests ✓ Works in airplane mode ✗ No INTERNET permission

You can verify this independently using any Android permission auditing tool.

Sentry contains no advertisements and no third-party tracking or analytics SDKs.

07

Data Retention & Deletion

▶

Because no data is ever collected, there is no data to retain or delete on our end.

Locally, Sentry produces cleaned image files in your gallery. You retain full ownership and control of these files. You may delete them at any time using your device's gallery or file manager. Uninstalling the App removes all App-related data from your device.
